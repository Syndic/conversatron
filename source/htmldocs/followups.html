Posting Follow-ups

 The Conversatron

By now you are wondering, "so, like, I can reply to all of my threads now? Sweet! Oh, wait, but why doesn't it always show up?"

Well, we here at The Conversatron, always aiming to suck more of your time into the blackhole that is the internet, have implemented a new feature called "follow-ups".
This lets you, the writer, queue up a reply to your own threads. We can choose to ignore your reply, make it visible, or shamelessly rip-off your ideas. Fun for all involved!

Please note that you may only have one reply queued up per thread at once. Posting a new reply replaces any existing one you may have posted.
Enjoy!
